Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters — here's how we protect it

Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Ducie Dev ("we", "our", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile games and visit duciedev.com. By using our services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Device information (model, operating system, unique device identifiers)
  • Gameplay data (scores, progress, session duration) to improve game experience
  • Crash reports and performance data for debugging purposes
  • Contact information you voluntarily provide through our contact form (name, email, message)

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve our games and website
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Analyze usage trends to enhance the user experience
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent activity or technical issues
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

4. Third-Party Services

Our games may integrate third-party SDKs and services (such as analytics providers and ad networks). These third parties have their own privacy policies governing their data practices. We encourage you to review their policies. We are not responsible for the data practices of third-party services.

5. Advertising

Some of our free games may display advertisements provided by third-party ad networks. These networks may use cookies or device identifiers to serve personalized ads based on your interests. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by applicable law. Contact form submissions are stored for up to 12 months and then deleted.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities
  • Data portability where applicable

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us via our Contact page.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our services after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at:

  • Email: support@duciedev.com
  • Website: duciedev.com
  • Contact form: duciedev.com/contact
